Researcher @ Harvard SEAS

Hi! I am Satya and I am a PhD student at Harvard School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS) working on trustworthy aspects of machine learning with Prof. Hima Lakkaraju and Prof. Finale Doshi-Velez. I also collaborate with Prof. Sameer Singh from UC, Irvine and Prof. Steven Wu from Carnegie Mellon.
Before starting my PhD, I led several research initiatives at Amazon for multiple products, including Amazon Web Services (AWS), Alexa and Amazon Search (A9). I completed my masters from the School of Computer Science (SCS) @ Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh Campus, with my research focus on building smart conversational agents.
